Company Refund & Cancellation Policy
------------------------------------

1. General Policy
Customers may request a refund for any eligible product or service purchased directly from our company. All refund requests must include proof of purchase.

2. Cancellations
A customer may cancel their order within 7 days of purchase for a full refund, provided the service has not yet been activated or the product has not been shipped.

If the service has already started, prorated refunds may be offered at the company’s discretion.

3. Non-Refundable Items
The following items are not eligible for cancellation or refunds:
- Digital downloads once the download link has been accessed
- Customized or personalized items
- Services already delivered in full

4. Processing Time
Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and issued back to the original payment method.

5. Contact
For refund or cancellation queries, reach out to support@company.com.
